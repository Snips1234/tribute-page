Elon Musk

Elon Much is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman who founded X.com in 1999 (which later became PayPal), SpaceX in 2002 and Tesla Motors in 2003. Much became a multimillionaire in his late 20s when he sold his start-up company, Zip2, to division of Compaq Computers. He has am estimated net-worth of around 59.2 billion dollars and is listed as the 16th richest person in the world according to Forbes.
